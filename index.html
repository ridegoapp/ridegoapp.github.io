FAQ

How much does it cost to rent bike?

Our bike rental prices start at $10 per hour. we also offer daily and weekly rental options at discounts rates.

Do I need to make a reservation?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend making a reservation in advance to ensure bike availability, especially during peak seasons

What is the minimum age requirement for renting a bike?

The minimum age requirement for renting a bike is 18 years old. Riders under 18 years old must be accompanied by a parent or guardian

Are helmets provided with the bike rental?

Yes, we provide helmets with every bike rental. Safety is our top priority, and we strongly encourage all riders to wear helmets.

What happens if the bike gets damaged during the rental period?

In case of any damages to the bike during the rental period, the renter will be responsible for the repair costs. We recommend checking the bike thoroughly before renting and reporting any existing damages to our staff
